About

Architect & Writer,
Revealing the Unseen

NI
Photo here

Architecture has always been about more than just buildings. I am an architect and writer trained at DUET, with a CGPA of 3.75, ranking first in my batch across nine consecutive semesters.

My thesis, "The Golden Fiber Tales," focused on the adaptive reuse of the historic Adamjee Jute Mill, serving as an act of cultural reclamation and giving voice to a place the city had forgotten.

My work exists at the crossroads of heritage, sustainability, and storytelling. A space, like a sentence, must resonate emotionally before it can be comprehended.
